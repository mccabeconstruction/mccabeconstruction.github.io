    About

    I started my construction career in 2010, shortly after moving to Canada from England. I’m practiced in various trades from tiling, framing, drywall, building envelopes and carpentry before landing in residential renovations. It is what I am truly passionate about and it allows me to utilize my variety of skills. I enjoy showcasing my eye for detail along with my understanding of the building project as a whole, and working directly with homeowners and designers. There’s nothing like seeing a project go from ideas on paper to a photo worthy finished product. I look forward to meeting with you and discussing you project!
